--- layout: default title: Home notitle: true --- The Scalable Computer Systems Lab develops efficient operating systems, storage systems, and distributed systems for emerging systems hardware and increasingly granular workloads.

Splinter team at OSDI'18 in San Diego (Sara Moore, Ryan Stutsman, Chinmay Kulkarni, and Mazhar Naqvi).

The Utah SCS lab works at the boundary between operating systems, storage systems, and computer architecture. We develop new approaches for speeding up software's interactions with I/O devices, techniques to exploit coherent interconnects in applications for persistent and disaggregated memory systems, and programmable storage systems that move computation closer to data while preserving safety and performance.
