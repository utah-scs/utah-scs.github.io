Splinter team at OSDI'18 in San Diego (Sara Moore, Ryan Stutsman, Chinmay Kulkarni, and Mazhar Naqvi).

The Utah SCS lab works at the boundary between operating systems, storage systems, and computer architecture. Recent work studies fast I/O and memory ordering for emerging interconnects, cache-coherent and disaggregated memory systems, and programmable storage systems that move computation closer to data while preserving safety and performance.
